How to use
  • Click any text in the white sheet to edit it.
  • To add a photo: copy an image into your clipboard and paste it (Ctrl+V) onto the photo box, or drag & drop an image into the sheet (many browsers allow this).
  • When ready, open Print (Ctrl+P) and choose "Save as PDF" to download your resume/portfolio.
Notes:
  • Pasting images or dragging files into the editable area often embeds them directly into the document (browser dependent).
  • This page uses only HTML and CSS so it works offline and is easy to customize.
